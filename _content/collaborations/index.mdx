

## what should this page contain?

- Silver Medal Criterion #2
  - Collaborate with one (or more) 2022 iGEM team(s) in a meaningful way.
- Which teams can we work with?
  - You can work with any other iGEM 2022 team in the competition. You can also work with non-iGEM research groups, but they do not count towards the iGEM team collaboration silver medal criterion.
- Some suggestions
  - Improve the function of another team's BioBrick Part or Device
  - Characterize another team's part
  - Debug a construct
  - Model or simulate another team's system
  - Test another team's software
